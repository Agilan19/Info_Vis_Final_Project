Toronto Bicycle Parking

We have chosen to do a problem-driven design study, for which we wish to investigate an ongoing problem. The problem we have decided to study is that certain wards in Toronto do not have access to bicycle parking in comparison to others so we would like to do a visualization which can be presented to the City of Toronto highlighting the many wards in Toronto which lack cycling access.
